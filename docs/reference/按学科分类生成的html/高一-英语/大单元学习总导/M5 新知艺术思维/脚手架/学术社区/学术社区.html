👥 同伴连接：英语讨论小组

话题："Is it easier to make friends online or offline? Why?"

小组分工：

  • 主持人 组织议程 + 计时，确保人人参与
  • 记录员 记录关键观点和结论
  • 发言人 总结小组观点并向全班汇报
  • 评价员 评估讨论质量和参与度
Useful Phrases:
"In my opinion, online friendships are easier because..."
"However, offline friendships might be deeper since..."
💡 先用1分钟独立思考，再用英语轮流发言

话题："What would Anne Frank write on her social media if she were alive today?"

产出：每个小组提交一份 150 词以上的英文讨论纪要

Starter:
"If Anne Frank had social media, I think she would post about..."
"She might use it to connect with the outside world because..."

⏱ 讨论时间：15分钟 | 汇报时间：每组3分钟

任务：两人一组，互读对方写的"My Friendship Manifesto"（友谊宣言），用 "One Star + One Wish"方式反馈。

One Star (一个亮点): "I really like how you described loyalty as..."
One Wish (一个建议): "Maybe you could add an example about..."
⭐ One Star: "Your definition of trust is very clear — I can really understand what you mean."
🌟 One Wish: "Maybe you could add one personal experience to make it more vivid."

🏠 家校连接：家庭采访任务

采访你的父母或祖父母（中文或英文沟通均可，但记录需用英文）：

  1. Who was your best friend when you were my age?
  2. How did you become friends?
  3. Has the meaning of friendship changed for you as you grew older?

📝 产出：英文短文 "My Family Member's Friendship Story: Then and Now"（100词以上）

My Father's Friendship Story: Then and Now
When my father was my age, his best friend was his classmate Li Wei. They became friends because they sat next to each other in class and both loved playing basketball...

🌍 社会连接：真实交流入口

📬 方案 A：国际笔友
通过学校国际部或 ePals 平台与英语母语同龄人成为笔友（中长期）
💬 方案 B：语言社区
在 FluentU / HiNative 发布关于 Friendship 的问题，收集跨国回答（短期）
🔴 方案 C：Reddit 讨论
参与 r/penpals 或 r/friendship 话题，用英语与陌生人交流友谊的看法（在线）

👤 角色分工表

角色 职责 关键英语用语
主持人 组织讨论流程，确保人人参与 "Let's start with...", "What's your opinion?"
记录员 记录关键观点和结论 "I'll summarize...", "The main points are..."
发言人 向全班汇报讨论结果 "Our group believes that..."
评价员 评估讨论质量和参与度 "One thing we did well: ..."
