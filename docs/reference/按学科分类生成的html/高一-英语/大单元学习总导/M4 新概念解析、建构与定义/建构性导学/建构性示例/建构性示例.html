🧭 My Friendship Compass

建构我的友谊理解模型 · 中英文双语建构

把你的理解组织成一个有结构的模型——它不仅帮你回答"What makes a real friendship?"，更让你能用英语清晰定义友谊。

📬 真实情境：跨国笔友的困惑

"Hey, I need your help. Jack and I used to be so close — we talked every day. But lately, he barely says hi. I feel confused and hurt. Is this still a friendship? What makes a real friendship?"
— Tom, your American pen pal

Tom 的问题没有"是或否"的简单答案。要帮他，你先为自己建构一套完整的友谊理解模型——然后才能用英语解释给他听。

📋 建构任务说明

成果名称：My Friendship Definition & Model

用途：回复 Tom 的邮件 + 展示在班级英语角

形式：视觉模型图（A4）+ 文字定义（8-12句）

📌 最低标准（验收条件）
  • ✅ 模型中至少包含 4 个 friendship 要素
  • ✅ 至少标注 2 组 要素间的逻辑关系
  • ✅ 文字定义中至少使用 5 个 本单元核心词汇
  • ✅ 文字定义有：主题句 → 展开论述 → 总结句
  • ✅ 视觉模型与文字定义保持一致

🌟 成果样例

模型类型：金字塔（由底到顶）

🏆 Support & Sharing
↑ Trust and respect create safety ↑
🤝 Trust & Mutual Respect
↑ Honest communication builds foundation ↑
💬 Honesty & Open Communication
In my understanding, friendship is built on a three-layer foundation. At the base, honesty and open communication allow friends to share their true thoughts without fear. On top of that, trust and mutual respect develop naturally. At the highest level, friendship means support and sharing — both happy and difficult moments. A sincere friend stands by you. True friendship is a bond of honesty, trust, and support that grows stronger over time.

↑ 此样例包含 5+ 核心词汇（加粗标注），对应金字塔的三层结构，符合最低标准。

📈 成果优化 & 自我评价

深度优化：在模型中加入 boundaries、individuality 等进阶要素；使用 furthermore、as a result 等连接词提升连贯性。

创意拓展：增加跨文化对比段落——"In Chinese culture, friendship may emphasize ___, while in Western culture, ___ is more valued."
