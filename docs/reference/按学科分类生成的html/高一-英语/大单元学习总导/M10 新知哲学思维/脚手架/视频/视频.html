Level 1: Basic — 理解与提取 Explanatory Viewing

🔍 Friendship Detectives

从 TED-Ed 动画视频中找出并记录交朋友的四步科学策略。边看边填"侦探记录表"。

🎯 Pre-viewing warm-up (3 min)
  • Think-Pair-Share: "What was the last time you made a new friend? How did it happen?"
  • Pre-teach vocabulary: approach, initiate, mutual, vulnerability, bond
❓ Key Questions
  1. What are the four key steps to making a new friend? List them in order.
  2. Why does the video say "proximity" is important for friendship?
  3. What is the role of "self-disclosure" in deepening a friendship?
⏸ Pause-and-Think Points

My Friendship Recipe Card — 设计一张"友谊配方卡"（60—80 词），总结视频中的四步策略加一条个人建议。至少使用三个预学词汇。
Level 2: Advanced — 分析与评价 Analytical Viewing

🔍 The Friendship Mythbuster

观看 Marisa Franco 的 TED 演讲，记录她提出的"成人交友"三大迷思以及对应的科学真相。

🎯 Pre-viewing warm-up (5 min)
  • Discussion: "Do you think making friends becomes harder as we grow up? Why?"
  • Vocabulary preview: assumption, scarcity mindset, taking initiative, rejection sensitivity
❓ Key Questions
  1. What is the "scarcity mindset" Franco describes? How does it stop us?
  2. Why does Franco argue that "taking initiative" is the single most important skill?
  3. What is the relationship between vulnerability and belonging?
⏸ Pause-and-Think Points
时间暂停点思考任务
3:20"We assume friendship happens naturally — it doesn't."Have you ever waited for friendship to "just happen"?
7:50演讲者搬到新城市感到孤独的故事What did she do differently? What does this tell us about initiative?
11:30"liking gap" 现象Have you ever underestimated how much someone liked you?

输出时使用以下短语：
The speaker argues that… / One key finding is… / This resonates with my experience because… / However, I wonder whether…

Level 3: Challenge — 批判与思辨 Debate Viewing

🔍 Cross-Cultural Friendship Case Study

观看 MOOC 课程片段，聚焦跨文化友谊中的沟通误解与调适策略。完成"高语境 vs 低语境文化"对比分析表。

🎯 Pre-viewing warm-up (5 min)
  • Think-Pair-Share: "What misunderstandings might happen with a friend from a different culture?"
  • Key concepts: high-context culture, low-context culture, direct vs. indirect communication, face-saving
❓ Key Questions
  1. How does the course distinguish between high-context and low-context communication?
  2. What misunderstandings arise when friends from these two styles interact?
  3. What three strategies are suggested for bridging cross-cultural gaps?
⏸ Pause-and-Think Points
时间暂停点思考任务
2:10日本学生和美国学生约见面Was the misunderstanding about time — or something deeper?
5:30"direct feedback" vs "indirect feedback"When is direct feedback better? When might it hurt?
8:15中国学生分享与西方朋友相处经验She had to learn to "say no directly." Why was that hard?
Cross-Cultural Friendship Playbook（150—180 词）
Part A: 高语境 vs 低语境友谊期望对比表（至少 3 个维度）
Part B: 一段短文回答 —— "If you were to befriend someone from a different culture, what three communication rules would you set?"

📝 元认知反思

完成以上观影后，选择至少 2 个问题用英语作答（每题 30—50 词）：

  1. 三个层级的观影任务中，哪一个视频对你的触动最大？为什么？
  2. 你在做"暂停思考"练习时，更容易在哪个方面深入思考？提取信息、分析观点，还是提出质疑？
  3. 观看前发的词汇，你在输出时实际用上了几个？哪些还需要更多练习？
  4. 让你给还没看过的同学写一句话推荐，你会说什么？
