📖 基础层 · 解释型视频

基础理解5-8 min

视频1：What Makes a True Friend?

🎯 观看任务：找出视频中提到的友谊的三个关键要素，并用英语记录下来。

⏸ 暂停思考点 — 02:35：Do you agree that trust is the foundation of all friendships? Why or why not?

❓ 关键问题：

  • What are the three essential elements of friendship according to the video?
  • How does the video define "true friendship"?
  • Can you think of a personal example that matches one of the elements?

🔍 进阶层 · 分析型视频

进阶分析8-12 min

视频2：How Friendships Evolve Over Time

🎯 观看任务：分析视频中一段友谊关系的发展过程，标出关键转折点。

❓ 关键问题：

  • What were the key turning points in the friendship described?
  • What factors caused the friendship to change over time?
  • How did the people involved adapt to these changes?

🚀 挑战层 · 辩论 / 前沿型视频

挑战批判10-15 min

视频3：TED Talk — The Science of Friendship

🎯 观看任务：关注研究者提出的最新发现，思考它如何挑战你对友谊的传统认知。

⏸ 暂停思考点 — 06:18：The speaker claims "your brain treats online friends the same as offline ones." Do you buy that?

❓ 关键问题：

  • What was the most surprising research finding in this talk?
  • How does the "Dunbar's number" relate to your own social circle?
  • What implications does this research have for how you manage friendships?
